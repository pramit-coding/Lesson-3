Streams

Mansfield State High School offers a variety of academic streams and programs to cater to different student interests and strength Academic Excellence Programs: These include specialized programs in Mathematics, Science, and English to challenge high-achieving students. French Immersion Program: This program allows students to learn various subjects in French,promoting bilingual proficiency. Music Program: A comprehensive music program that includes instrumental music, choir, and various ensembles. Technology and Digital Solutions: Courses that focus on modern technology, coding, and digital literacy. Learning Support and Life Skills: Programs designed to support students with additional learning needs and to develop essential life skills.

Registered Education

Mansfield State High School is registered under the Queensland Department of Education1. This means it follows the ciurriculum and guidelines set by the Queensland Curriculum and Assessment Authority (QCAA).
